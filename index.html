Our Story

Crafting Excellence,
One Stitch at a Time

GH Fashion Empire was born from a deep passion for fashion and a relentless commitment to quality. Based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, we blend traditional artistry with contemporary design to create garments that transcend trends and endure through time.

From the first consultation to the final fitting, every piece we create is a personal statement, tailored to your unique shape, personality, and occasion. We dress men and women who refuse to compromise on excellence.

Our Mission

To make every client feel extraordinary through exceptional craftsmanship and personalized style.

Our Vision

To be West Africa's most trusted premium fashion house, celebrated for quality and innovation.

Meet the Visionary

Ikechi Gift Chijioke

Founder & Creative Director

I am a fashion designer who makes all kinds of traditional wears, suits, senators, custom wears and high-class designer's wear for both male and female. My passion is helping each client discover the power of dressing with intention because when you look exceptional, you feel exceptional, and the world responds in kind.

Fashion Design Training

Whether you are picking up a needle for the first time or ready to turn your passion into a business — our training programmes are designed to take you from where you are to where you want to be. Learn directly from Ikechi Gift Chijioke with over 10 years of hands-on experience.

Beginner Programme

Never held a needle before? No problem. This programme starts from absolute zero — you will learn how to handle fabrics, understand measurements, thread a machine, and sew your very first garment. Perfect for anyone who has always dreamed of making their own clothes.

  • ✦ Introduction to fabrics and materials
  • ✦ Body measurements and sizing
  • ✦ Basic hand and machine sewing
  • ✦ Your first complete outfit
Enroll Now →

Advanced Programme

Already know the basics and ready to go further? This programme dives deep into professional techniques used in high-end fashion ateliers. You will master complex cuts, luxury finishes, and multi-garment collections that can compete at any level.

  • ✦ Advanced cutting and draping techniques
  • ✦ Luxury finishing and lining
  • ✦ Bridal and evening wear construction
  • ✦ Building a signature style
Enroll Now →

Pattern Making

Pattern making is the backbone of every great garment. In this programme you will learn how to draft, grade, and alter patterns from scratch — giving you the power to create any silhouette your client desires without relying on ready-made templates.

  • ✦ Drafting patterns from measurements
  • ✦ Grading patterns across sizes
  • ✦ Alterations and adjustments
  • ✦ Digital and manual pattern techniques
Enroll Now →

Fashion Business

Talent alone does not build a business — strategy does. This programme teaches you how to price your work, attract clients, build your brand, manage orders, and grow a sustainable fashion business right here in Nigeria and beyond.

  • ✦ Pricing and profit margins
  • ✦ Client management and retention
  • ✦ Branding and social media for fashion
  • ✦ Scaling from freelance to atelier
Enroll Now →
