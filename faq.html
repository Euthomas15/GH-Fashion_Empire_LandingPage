Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing an order with GH Fashion Empire.

Do I need an appointment before visiting?

Yes. We recommend booking an appointment in advance so we can dedicate enough time to discuss your design, take accurate measurements, and provide personalized service.

How long does it take to complete an outfit?

Production time depends on the design and level of craftsmanship required. Most outfits are completed within 3–14 business days, while bridal and luxury pieces may require additional time.

Can I bring my own fabric?

Absolutely. Clients are welcome to provide their own fabric. Our team will inspect the material to ensure it is suitable for the chosen design.

Do you offer nationwide delivery?

Yes. We deliver within Port Harcourt and can arrange secure nationwide delivery across Nigeria through trusted logistics partners.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept bank transfers and other approved payment methods communicated by GH Fashion Empire.

Still Need Help?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, our team is ready to assist you. Contact GH Fashion Empire today and we'll be happy to help.
