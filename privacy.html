Privacy Policy

Learn how GH Fashion Empire collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

Privacy Policy

At GH Fashion Empire, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our tailoring and fashion design services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, delivery address, body measurements, and any additional information you provide when making an enquiry, booking an appointment, or placing an order.

Website Information

We may automatically collect technical information such as your browser type, device information, IP address, and pages visited to improve the performance and security of our website.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process tailoring and custom fashion orders.
  • Schedule appointments and fittings.
  • Communicate with you regarding your enquiries or orders.
  • Arrange delivery of completed outfits.
  • Improve our website, products, and customer experience.
  • Send promotional offers only when you have agreed to receive them.

Sharing Your Information

GH Fashion Empire does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may only share your information with trusted delivery partners or service providers when necessary to complete your order or comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyse website traffic, and enhance our services. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Any updates will be published on this page with the revised effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your personal information is handled, please contact GH Fashion Empire through the contact details provided on our website. We will be happy to assist you.

Still Need Help?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, our team is ready to assist you. Contact GH Fashion Empire today and we'll be happy to help.
